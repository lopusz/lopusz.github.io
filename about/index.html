About Me

I work as a data scientist at ICM, University of Warsaw.

I hold a PhD in theoretical physics from Faculty of Physics, University of Warsaw. My research were carried out within the framework of Polish-German PhD studies organised jointly by ICM and IWR, University of Heidelberg. I specialised in Monte Carlo simulations of complex materials.
